• Easy Menu Iceberg Lettuce with Fish
    • Ingredients:
    • 1 small iceberg lettuce, teared
    • 300 g Fish
    • Dip; garlic, red onion, salt, pound chilli and sugar

    • Cooking
    • Step 1
    • Air-frier, 200 degree celsius. Grill the fish approx 10-15 mins till golden brown. Put on the iceberg lettuce.
    • Step 2
    • Sprinkle with dip
    • Deep Fried garlic
    • red onion
    • Then mix it with salt
    • pound chilli and sugar.
